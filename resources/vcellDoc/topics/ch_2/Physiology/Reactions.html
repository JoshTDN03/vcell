Reactions

Reactions tab defines regular reactions and reactions rules in the same tabular interface. Regular reactions act on species (variables) located in compartments or on a membrane and defined by kinetic laws for compartment reactions or membrane fluxes.

Create, view and edit reactions and membrane fluxes using either the tabular or the diagrammatic views. Properties for reactions and fluxes can be found in the Reactions properties pane or Membrane Flux properties pane in the bottom of the window.

Reactions rules act on species patterns (classes of species having same features), thus they define a class of reactions. Individual reactions are generated by applying reactions rules to the set of initial species. They can be seen within Application, but are not displayed in Reaction Diagram.

Create, view and edit reaction rules using properties paneonly in the bottom of the window.
