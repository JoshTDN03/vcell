Protocols Overview

Protocols are additional modeling capabilities that are available to users to enhance their models. Protocols are specified at the application level.



Protocols can be accessed by clicking the Protocols item under any Application in the 'Navigation' panel tree structure. This selects the Protocols tab for the application in the 'View' panel on the right. The 'Protocols' tab has the following 2 sub-tabs.

  • Events
  • Electrical Mapping
