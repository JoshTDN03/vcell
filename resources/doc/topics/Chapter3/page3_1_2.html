3.1.2 Adjusting FRAP Experimental Data
The adjustment toolbox in Virtual Frap provides basic image processing functions. These functions are described below.

Zoom in - To zoom in the image, users should click on the zoom in button . Each clicking on the zoom in button will make the image one size bigger.

Zoom out - To zoom out the image, users should click on the zoom out button . Each clicking on the zoom out button will make the image one size smaller.

Increase contrast - To increase the image contrast, users should click on the increase contrast button . Each clicking on the increase contrast button will make the image contrast one size bigger.

Reduce contrast - To reduce the image contrast, users should click on the reduce contrast button . Each clicking on the reduce contrast button will make the image contrast one size smaller.

Crop - There are two ways to crop the image. One is customized crop which uses should click on crop button and click on the image and drag the mouse to draw the desired rectangular crop window. Another way to crop image is by clicking on automatically crop button and the image will be cropped by a default crop window.
